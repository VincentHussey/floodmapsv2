Floods Directive

The Commissioners of Public Works have been appointed as the competent authority to implement the Floods Directive in Ireland.

Preliminary Flood Risk assessment (PFRA)

A Preliminary Flood Risk assessment was completed in 2011 to identify areas for further assessment under the Floods Directive. This assessment was based on existing or readily available data.
